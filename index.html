About Jocale

Handcrafted fine jewelry for the everyday.

We believe luxury should be accessible, fun, and part of your everyday. New, high-quality, modern, minimalistic earrings, necklaces, bracelets, and rings are made available to her every week.

Intended Users

Why Jocale?

Latin word for "jewelry". We work with top jewelers, who commit to exceptional craftsmanship and ethical practices, to produce quality essentials. We free ourselves from middlemen and sell directly to you, so, we can provide fine jewelry at a fraction of the price. It is our mission to have women embrace a daily dose of luxury. These strategies make the brand stand out in a crowded marketplace.

Details & Materials

Our diamonds are high-quality (SI 1-2 clarity) and ethically sourced from conflict-free suppliers who follow socially responsible practices, making it both conscious and durable.

Our 14k solid gold pieces are made to last forever. Since gold is a precious metal, it scratches less, doesn’t bend or wear out easily and won’t oxidize so you can wear it everyday and everywhere - even the shower.
